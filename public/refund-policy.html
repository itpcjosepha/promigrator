ProMigrator 2FM - Refund Policy

Last updated: April 22, 2026

Our Promise: We stand behind ProMigrator 2FM. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee.

1. 30-Day Refund Guarantee

We offer a full refund of your subscription fee if you request it within 30 days of your purchase, for any reason or no reason at all. No questions asked.

2. Eligibility

You are eligible for a refund if:

  • Your request is made within 30 days of the original purchase date
  • Your account is in good standing (no violations of Terms of Service)
  • You purchased directly from ProMigrator or through our authorized merchant of record (Paddle or LemonSqueezy)

3. How to Request a Refund

Step 1: Contact Support

Email our support team at support@promigrator.com with "Refund Request" in the subject line and include:

  • Your email address associated with the purchase
  • The order date
  • Brief reason for your refund request (optional but helpful)
Step 2: Confirmation

We will verify your purchase and confirm your eligibility. This typically takes 1-2 business days.

Step 3: Processing

Once approved, we will process the refund to your original payment method. Refunds typically appear within 5-10 business days depending on your payment provider.

4. Trial to Paid Conversion

Special Case: If you convert from a 30-day trial to a paid subscription, your purchase date is the conversion date, not the trial start date. You have 30 days from conversion to request a refund.

5. Renewal Refunds

If your subscription was automatically renewed and you request a refund:

  • Within 30 days of renewal: Full refund of the renewal fee
  • After 30 days: Refund may not be available, but we encourage you to contact us to discuss alternatives

6. What is NOT Covered

Refunds may not be available for:

  • Requests made more than 30 days after purchase
  • Purchases made more than 30 days ago that are requesting a refund now (unless within the renewal window)
  • Accounts with violations of our Terms of Service
  • Multiple refund requests for the same purchase
  • Purchases made through unauthorized resellers or third-party marketplaces

7. Refund Method

Refunds are issued to your original payment method. If your original payment method is no longer available, we will work with you to arrange an alternative refund method.

8. Access After Refund

Once a refund is processed:

  • Your license will be downgraded to ToolBox (free tier) or deactivated
  • You will lose access to paid features
  • You may continue to use the free tier (ToolBox) without additional charges

9. Partial Refunds

In rare circumstances where you request a refund outside the 30-day window, we may consider a partial refund based on:

  • Time elapsed since purchase
  • Reason for your request
  • Your account history

Contact support to discuss your specific situation.

10. Chargebacks

If you initiate a chargeback instead of requesting a refund through this process, we reserve the right to:

  • Permanently disable your license and account
  • Decline future purchases
  • Recover associated fees from the chargeback process

We prefer to resolve disputes through this refund policy rather than chargeback processes.

11. Grace Period Policy

Our 7-day grace period after license expiration is not part of this refund policy. The grace period allows read-only access to your work. Refunds cannot be requested retroactively for expired licenses.

12. Contact Us

To request a refund or discuss your situation, please contact:

  • Email: support@promigrator.com
  • Subject: "Refund Request"
  • Response Time: 1-2 business days

13. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Refund Policy. Any changes will be communicated to you via email or in-app notification. Your continued use of the Software constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Questions? We want you to be satisfied with ProMigrator. If you have questions about refunds or anything else, don't hesitate to reach out to our support team.
