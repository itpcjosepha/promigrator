ProMigrator 2FM - Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 22, 2026

1. Overview

ProMigrator is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Software.

Key Point: ProMigrator 2FM operates locally on your computer. Your FileMaker databases and migration data never leave your machine unless you explicitly upload them or choose to use optional cloud features.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, name, and organization (for subscription and support purposes)
  • Billing Information: Processed through our payment provider (Paddle or LemonSqueezy). We do not store credit card details.
  • Support Information: If you contact us for support, we collect the information you provide

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymized usage statistics (features used, session duration) to improve the Software
  • System Information: OS version, architecture (macOS/Windows), for compatibility and support purposes
  • Error Reports: Crash logs and error traces to help us improve stability (fully optional, requires your consent)
  • License Activation: Machine fingerprint (hardware ID) for license validation and machine limit enforcement

2.3 Information NOT Collected

  • Your FileMaker database content
  • Your migration data or file contents
  • Your credentials, keys, or encryption passwords
  • Your project files or local workspace data

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for:

  • Providing and maintaining the Software
  • Processing subscription payments and renewals
  • Sending service announcements and support communications
  • Responding to your inquiries and support requests
  • Improving Software stability and features
  • Enforcing license terms and preventing fraud
  • Compliance with legal obligations

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Payment Processors: Paddle or LemonSqueezy (for billing and subscription management)
  • Email Providers: For sending transactional emails (activation keys, password resets, support)
  • Legal Authorities: If required by law or to protect rights and safety

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all communications
  • Secure database practices with encryption at rest
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Restricted access to personal data on a need-to-know basis

However, no system is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to provide the Software and fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. You may request deletion of your data at any time (subject to legal retention requirements).

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of non-essential communications
  • Data portability (receive your data in a portable format)

To exercise these rights, contact us through our support page.

8. Cookies and Tracking

The Software itself does not use cookies. Our website may use cookies for:

  • Session management
  • User preferences
  • Analytics (anonymized, aggregated)

You can control cookie settings through your browser.

9. Third-Party Links

Our website or Software may contain links to third-party sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

10. Children's Privacy

ProMigrator is not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If we learn we have collected information from a child, we will delete it promptly.

11. GDPR and International Privacy

If you are located in the European Union or United Kingdom, your data is processed in accordance with GDPR. You have additional rights including the right to lodge a complaint with your data protection authority.

12. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be communicated to you via email or in-app notification. Continued use of the Software constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

13. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us through our support page.
